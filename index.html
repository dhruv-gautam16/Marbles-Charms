Our Story

Meet Marble, the face behind the charms

Marble Charms started as a tiny side table of beads, bows and jump rings — now it's a one-person, one-dog studio making charms meant to be noticed.

Every charm is handmade in small batches, which means no two are ever quite the same, and every personalised order gets the same care as the very first one.

No storefront, no checkout page — just a chat. Tell us what you're picturing, and we'll make it real.
